Our Services

Reliable Solutions For Your Hydraulic Needs

Total Hose Solution

Alwafra Hydraulic is committed to provide a complete hydraulic solution for our customers

Exclusive Retail Shop

Our retail shop is open six days a week. Customers will be able to select from a comprehensive range of hoses and accessories.

Tube, Pipe Bending & Installation

We offer expertise in on-site bending and fabrication for hydraulic tubing (ranging from 6mm to 42mm & 1/4" to 1.1/4")

Hose Cleaning
